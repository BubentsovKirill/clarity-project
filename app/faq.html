Questions and answers

  1. Why is dating.clarity-project.net and how we differ from other dating sites?

    The main difference of dating.clarity-project.net from other dating sites is that we offer the services of professional translators that help men in communicating with Slavic girls who do not know the foreign languages. Unfortunately, many girls from the former Soviet Union do not know a foreign language at a high level, but nevertheless they want to marry a foreigner and live abroad. We understand that the only way to choose the right life partner is in full understanding of each other. This can not be achieved by using the online translator. That’s why we cooperate with the translation company that offers the professional translations Online-Translate.biz.

  2. How can I write a letter to a girl on the site?

    There are two ways how to write the letter and, in general, communicate on the site:
    - to use the services of our partner Online-Translate.biz and order the translations of the messages;
    - to get Active Membership and write the messages without the translations.

  3. How can I use the translation services?

    While registering in dating.clarity-project.net you automatically get the account in Online-Translate.biz with the same login and password as in dating.clarity-project.net. To add funds to your account and start using the services of professional translators follow the link Online-Translate.biz, enter the same login and password as in dating.clarity-project.net and there you can choose any payment system suitable for you. After the payment you can order the translations of the messages in dating.clarity-project.net. Your balance is always visible in dating.clarity-project.net and Online-Translate.biz.

  4. I do not want to order the translations of the messages, what should I do?

    For those men who are not willing to use the services of professional translators, we offer another option - to get an Active Membership. This means that you can write an unlimited number of emails to any girls on the site without the translation. To become an Active Member, you need to click on "Get Active Membership" that is located in your profile on dating.clarity-project.net.

  5. What options are available for me after getting Active Membership?

    - 10% discount at Online-Translate.biz;
    - a possibility to send messages (an original message without translation is sent);
    - the ability to see "my matches";
    - the ability to see "my visitors."

  6. How can girls register in the site? Where is the guarantee that I correspond with a real girl?

    Unfortunately, the Internet is full of scammers and fake profiles who have their own selfish goals. We try to protect our members from all kind of fraud. All the girls who want to register in our site are reviewed by our managers via a telephone call or a copy of the document proving her identity. After registration each girl is given only 2 invitation codes. This is for safety measures and helps to avoid different scammers and inadequate persons.
