Type of temperament

Temperament is something we were born with and something we live all our life with. It is the foundation of our character. Such things as how we get along with people and how we act in different situations depend on our temperament. Choleric, sanguine, phlegmatic, melancholic - whom will you be comfortable with? To find this out, defining your temperament would be a good start.

  • In a new situation I quickly adjust to new conditions
  • I easily adapt to other people
  • I get very nervous speaking in public
  • I am afraid of heavy rains and thunders
  • On vacation I easily adapt to the new order of the day
  • Sounds coming from a dentist's office worry me
  • I am usually good at catching thrown things
  • I often feel embarrassed
  • While watching a football game, it is difficult for me to follow the player's movements
  • I easily get excited
